# 1. Brand Overview

GAGI SACCO is a trusted supplier of high-quality precast concrete products and construction materials in Murang'a County, Kenya. The company's mission is to provide durable, reliable, and affordable products that support residential, commercial, and road construction projects.

---

# 2. Brand Personality

The GAGI SACCO brand should always communicate:

- Quality
- Professionalism
- Strength
- Community Development
